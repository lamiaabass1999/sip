Types of triangles and their properties

Equilateral

  • All sides are equal
  • All angles are equal to 60
  • All angles add up to 180

Isosceles

  • Two sides are equal
  • Two angles are equal
  • All angles add up to 180

Scalene

  • No sides are equal
  • No angles are equal
  • All angles add up to 180
